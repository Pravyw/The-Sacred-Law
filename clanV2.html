The Sacred Law

I. HONOR ABOVE ALL
Treat every member with respect. Insults, hate, or drama are forbidden.
II. SPEAK WITH WISDOM
Use meaningful words. Spamming, trolling, or random chaos breaks the balance.
III. LOYALTY TO THE CLAN
Support the clan in wars, events, and decisions. Your loyalty maintains the scales.
IV. RESPECT THE HIERARCHY
Leaders and officers are judges — follow guidance respectfully.
V. KEEP PEACE
Conflicts must be solved with words, not fights. Officers will judge serious disputes.
VI. PROTECT ALLIES
Respect and support our allies. Disrespecting them harms the clan.
VII. STAY ACTIVE
Inactive members risk removal. Presence is part of the balance.
VIII. SACRED CONDUCT
No NSFW, harassment, or illegal links. The clan is a safe and divine space.
IX. UPHOLD THE BALANCE
Every action matters — fairness, loyalty, and respect keep the clan strong.
X. USE CHANNELS PROPERLY
Keep chats in the right channels. Random posts disrupt order.
XI. NO SELF-PROMOTION
Advertising or promoting other servers without permission is forbidden.
XII. OBEY THE LAW
The clan’s rules are sacred. Breaking them may lead to warning or removal.
